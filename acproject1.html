Title: The Development of Rebong Bakery Ordering System (Final Year Project) (2022)

The development of the Rebong Bakery Ordering System was my final year project while pursuing my degree, which I completed towards the end of 2022. This website streamlines bakery operations by allowing customers to browse products, check availability, and place orders in advance, eliminating the need for in-person visits. It also includes an admin interface for managing inventory, adding new products, and updating stock levels. This project enhanced my skills in web development, database management, and system design, providing practical experience in creating user-centric e-commerce solutions tailored to real-world business needs.
