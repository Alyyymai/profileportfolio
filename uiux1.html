OrganizeMe : To-Do List
The Product

OrganizeMe is a simple app that helps you create and manage to-do lists for all your tasks.

The Brief

I am a busy person who tries to remember everything, including meetings and house chores. Sometimes when I get distracted by something, it is easy for me to forget the important things. Even though I have written reminders on paper, I still cannot remember what should I do when I arrive home. To keep reminding me of the tasks, I use the Notes on my phone. However, the Notes application cannot remind me if I have already done the tasks or not. The app is only for writing down tasks; it doesn't provide reminders.

My phone’s Notepad to-do list feature interface.

I downloaded several apps promising to help me create to-do lists and organize my life. However, I found myself either struggling to understand how to use the application or getting distracted by the additional features the apps offered beyond basic to-do functionality.

I decided to design a To-do list app that:

  1. Extremely user-friendly.
  2. Easy to understand.
  3. Allows me to set reminders for every task.
  4. Enable me to create a To-do list for other days.

Design Solution
Sketching

Before I started designing on Figma, I sketched down a design solution using a pen and paper, So I could easily figure out how to begin my design on Figma. With a simple design, I fit in the solution that I have come up with in one paper.

Sketches of the to-do list app.

Wireframe

After sketching the design that I wanted, I jumped on Figma and designed wireframes with more detail and structure. After that, I focused more on colours and images to make the design more realistic.

Final Design
Conclusion

Designing for myself has helped me understand how to design a product that meets an exact need. There were times in the design process were I wanted to add features that I felt I would need but going back to my stated goals and needs when using this app and being aware of my behavior as the sole user, I stuck with the ‘blue print’. If I took a step further and redesigned the app for more users other than myself, there will be need for User research. Qualitative user research to be precise, I would host interviews with potential users. I would create Personas and design a solution based on the information I have gotten from the research. There would be testing of the prototype to improve the product.
